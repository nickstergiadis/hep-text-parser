Home Exercise Program Generator

A simple, free tool for physiotherapists and trainers to build polished home exercise programs from rough notes.

How it works
  1. Enter the patient details and optional program title/date.
  2. Paste your rough exercise list in plain language (e.g. “leg press 3x10 hold 5 sec”).
  3. Click Generate Exercises to convert your notes into editable exercise cards.
  4. Review or adjust the dosage, instructions, notes and video links for each exercise.
  5. Once satisfied, click Preview Program to see a patient‑ready layout.
  6. Finally, download/print as a PDF, copy a summary, or open an email draft to send directly to your client.
